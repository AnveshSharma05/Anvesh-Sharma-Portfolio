Line Following Car

Dec 2024

The line following car was designed as a project for EK210. Creating the car taught us design principles and the engineering process. Using our problem definition we decided on our solution using a pairwise comparison chart, defined metrics for our objectives, and a glassbox functional analysis to determine the functions in our solution.

These were not made by me alone but by the entire EK 210, Group 4, Fall 2025 semester.

The circuit diagram was not designed by me but I want to show that we designed it before actually constructing it.

And here is the cutting file I designed to become the acrylic plate chassis after laser cutting.
